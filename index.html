My favorite vacation spot

Goa is one of my favorite vacation spots in India.Goa is famous for it beaches. Baga beach and Anjuna beach are two most crowded beaches of goa.North goa is most famous among tourists whereas south goa is famous for its private beaches and serenity. Goa has many water sports like speedboating,kayaking along with adventure sports like bungee jumping.

Favorite activities at Goa

  1. Scuba Diving
  2. Para Sailing
  3. Bungee jumping
  4. Speed boating

Dishes that are a must try!

  • Fish fingers
